# Asian Oil Market Analysis (SQL + Power BI)

## 📌 Overview

This project analyzes fuel pricing dynamics across Asian countries by studying how global crude oil price movements influence domestic retail fuel prices. It highlights the role of taxation and subsidies in shaping final consumer prices.

---

## 🎯 Objectives

* Measure price pass-through from crude to retail fuel
* Analyze the impact of taxes and subsidies
* Study price convergence/divergence across countries
* Identify shock absorption vs amplification behavior

---

## 🧰 Tools & Technologies

* **SQL (MySQL)** – Data cleaning, transformation, and analysis
* **Power BI** – Interactive dashboard and visualization

---

## 📊 Key cards

1. **Avg Crude Price (USD/L)** – Global price benchmark
2. **Avg Retail Price (USD/L)** – Consumer fuel price
3. **Pass-Through Ratio** – Transmission of crude price changes
4. **Shock Absorption (ΔRetail − ΔCrude)** – Policy impact indicator

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* Crude oil prices remain consistent globally, while retail prices vary significantly by country
* Taxation has a stronger influence on retail prices than subsidies
* Some countries absorb global shocks, while others pass them fully to consumers
* Price divergence increased during high volatility periods (e.g., 2021–2022)
